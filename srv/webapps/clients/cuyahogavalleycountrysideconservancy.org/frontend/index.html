Our Mission

The Cuyahoga Valley Countryside Conservancy works to preserve farmland, support local farmers, and strengthen the connection between our community and the agricultural heritage of the Cuyahoga Valley. Through conservation easements, farmer support programs, and community engagement, we ensure that working farms remain a vital part of our landscape for generations to come.

What We Do

Land Conservation

Protecting farmland through conservation easements and sustainable land management practices.

Farmers Market

Supporting local farmers through the Countryside Farmers Market, connecting producers with the community.

Education & Outreach

Providing educational programs and resources to help people understand and appreciate local agriculture.

Farmer Support

Offering resources, technical assistance, and networking opportunities for local farmers.
